Biography

Early Life

Elton John was born Reginald Kenneth Dwight on March 25, 1947, in Pinner,
Middlesex, England. From a young age, he showed remarkable talent for music,
teaching himself to play the piano by ear. Supported by his mother but
discouraged by his strict father, Elton found comfort and expression through
music, performing at family gatherings and local pubs.
